JSplacement: 2 - Custom sprites

To create custom sprites you will need an illustration software capable of exporting SVG files such as Adobe Illustrator, Affinity Designer, or Inkscape. Please se attached image as a reference for creating your own sprites.

Start creating your custom sprite by opening a new document.
Due to way JSplacement: 2 sprite scaling has been set, the canvas should ALWAYS be in 1:1 ratio
to avoid any issues with scaling, proportions, and tiling when using seamless generator.
The base color of JSplacement: 2 maps is rgb(128,128,128). Any higher value will go up, lower will go down.

After drawing your sprite, scale it up or down to fit onto the canvas for the best result. For the reference, see the image on the right.
However, sprites that are intended to be smaller than the most can be smaller than the canvas itself.
Center aligning your sprite is not required. That one is up to your personal preference.

Import your sprites into JSplacement: 2 by opening user data folder in your home directory ("JSplacement"), or by pressing C on your keyboard.
Move your SVG sprites into "CustomSprites" folder and select "Custom Sprites" in JSplacement: 2's settings.


Warning: While using PNG sprites with JSplacement: 2 is possible, SVG is much lighter and prefered option.
